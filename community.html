WiredTiger community and contact information

MongoDB Inc. and the WiredTiger community maintain and develop WiredTiger on GitHub. All source code and revision histories for this software are available there, and all contributors are welcome!

Please submit any feature suggestions and bug reports at WiredTiger's JIRA Issues page.

Discussion of WiredTiger issues and development can be also be found in the WiredTiger Google Group.
